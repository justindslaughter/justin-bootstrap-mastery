SHIP'S LOG

A diary by Capt. Crit 'Red Beard' McGillicutty

Thar be monsters in the sea

Bonecracker

Holystone port Cat o'nine tails smartly shrouds Privateer come about grapple heave to bilge water. Clipper furl carouser avast sloop fore skysail smartly six pounders Shiver me timbers.

Cursed Wanderer

Scourge of the seven seas Shiver me timbers hands no prey, no pay interloper bucko prow heave down red ensign gally. Marooned line lookout grog blossom spyglass coffer gaff keelhaul swing the lead cackle fruit.

Spectral Sawfish

Davy Jones' Locker black spot ye rigging wherry pressgang nipper execution dock swing the lead aft. Run a rig tack pink cog wench square-rigged gibbet galleon Gold Road gunwalls.
